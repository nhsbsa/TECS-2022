What happens next?

After your maternity exemption certificate application has been submitted, an automatic check is carried out. You do not need to contact us.

You can apply for a maternity exemption cetificate if, at the time you claimed the free prescription (the date on your PCN), you:

  • were pregnant
  • had given birth in the previous 12 months

If your application is successful and you pay the £9.65 prescription charge your PCN will be closed. You'll not need to do anything more.
