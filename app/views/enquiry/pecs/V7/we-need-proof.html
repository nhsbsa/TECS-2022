How to send your proof

You can send your proof by email or post. If you send proof by email the maximum file size is 6 megabytes (MB).

Sending by email

Take a photo, scan or screenshot of your proof. If you're taking a photo, use a mobile phone or digital camera.

Check the image is clear and you can read the information, then send it as an email attachment to: nhsbsa.pecs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

You must include your:

  • reference number 11193492846
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

Once we receive your email, we will check your proof and contact you within 2 working days.

Sending by post

Send a photocopy of your proof to us and include your:

  • reference number 11193492846
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode
