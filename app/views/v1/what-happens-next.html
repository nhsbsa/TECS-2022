What happens next?

We will ask you a few simple questions. It may help to have your exemption certificate or benefits information ready.

We'll just:

  1. Ask for some details about your exemption or benefit.

  2. Ask about other exemptions you may have had, as this may be quicker for us to check.

  3. Tell you how to send us proof if we cannot confirm your exemption straight away.

  4. Let you know the next steps.
