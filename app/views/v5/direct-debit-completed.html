Direct Debit Completed

You have set up a Direct Debit to pay your penalty charge

Confirmation of the instruction will be sent to you in 3 working days, or be received by you no later than 10 working days before the first collection.

First Payment

Your first payment of £35.20 will be taken on or just after 01 February 2023.

Next Payment

Your next payment of £35.20 will be taken on or just after 01 March 2023.

As long as you keep up payments there will be no further charges added to your account.

How to avoid penalties in the future

  • Make sure that your GP and dentist have your correct home address
  • If you receive benefits or have an exemption certificate, make sure that the relevant organisations have your correct home address.
  • If you have an exemption certificate, make sure it does not expire.
  • For more information visit www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/penaltycharges
