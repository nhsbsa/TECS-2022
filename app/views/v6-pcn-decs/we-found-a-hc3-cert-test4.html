How to avoid a future Penalty Charge Notice

We match exemptions automatically using a combination of your:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Date of birth
  • NHS Number

Make sure these are correct at your GP surgery, dental practice and any benefits organisations.

Contact us to check the name and address on your NHS exemption.

If any of your details are not up to date, you may receive another Penalty Charge Notice in the future.

If you were not entitled to free or reduced cost dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made the claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
