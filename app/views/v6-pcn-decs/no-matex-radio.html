You did not have a valid maternity exemption certificate at the time, but you can still apply

Although you did not have a valid maternity exemption certificate when you claimed a free NHS prescription, you still have the chance to apply.

You have until 10 July 2023 to get a maternity exemption certificate.

If your application is successful, you'll only have to pay the original prescription charge. You will not have to pay your penalty charge.

What do you want to do next?

If you were not entitled to free or reduced cost NHS dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made this claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
