Asking for a review

We understand that you may have made an honest mistake and did not mean to do anything wrong.

If you have an exceptional reason why you claimed a free NHS prescription you were not entitled to, you can ask for a review.

Reasons we will not usually accept

We will not usually review your Penalty Charge Notice if:

  • your exemption certificate had expired
  • you feel that you were misadvised by your GP or by pharmacy staff
  • you thought that your benefit entitled you to claim
  • you thought your prescription prepayment certificate would automatically renew

What to do next

If you have an exceptional reason why you made the claim at the time, contact us to review your Penalty Charge Notice.

When you contact us you must include your:

  • reference number 11193492846
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

If the review is successful

Your penalty charge will be reduced or removed. You will still need to pay the original prescription charge.

If the review is unsuccessful

You will need to pay both the penalty charge and the original prescription charge.
