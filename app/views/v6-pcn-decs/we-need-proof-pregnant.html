Confirm your entitlement

We need proof that you were pregnant when you claimed free dental treatment, or had given birth in the previous 12 months

Important: Important information

You now have 60 days from today to send us your proof.

Type of proof we need to see

  • a MatB1 form
  • a notification of Birth form (issued by a registered midwife)
  • a Birth or still birth certificate
  • your maternity exemption certificate

How to send your proof

You can send your proof by email or post. If you send proof by email the maximum file size is 6 megabytes (MB).

Sending by email

Take a photo, scan or screenshot of your proof. If you're taking a photo, use a mobile phone or digital camera.

Check the image is clear and you can read the information, then send it as an email attachment to: nhsbsa.decs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

You must include your:

  • reference number 11193492846
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

Once we receive your email, we will check your proof and contact you within 2 working days.

Sending by post

Send a photocopy of your proof to us and include your:

  • reference number 11193492846
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

Send the copy of your proof to:

Information:

Dental Exemption Checking Team
NHSBSA
Bridge House
152 Pilgrim Street
Newcastle Upon Tyne
NE1 6SN

Once we receive your proof by post, we will check it and contact you within 5 working days.

If you were not entitled to free or reduced cost NHS dental treatment but have an exceptional reason why you made this claim, you can ask us to review your Penalty Charge Notice. Find out if you can ask for a review.
