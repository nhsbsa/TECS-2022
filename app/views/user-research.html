Hypothesis A

We believe by adding in content or a page around why the user cannot pay at the enquiry stage of the journey will help the user understand that they will have to wait until they get a PCN. This is in hopes to cause less frustration and confusion. We understand from our CSAT results that this is one of the biggest issues the user faces with this journey.

On the view PCN page, users would not be able to view previous payments made and how this effected their total. We therefore decided to add this functionality to allow users to know how much they have left to pay, and be assured that their previous partial payments have been tracked

We also added in an extra screen into pay by card which gives users the option to make a full or partial payment.

What we created:

  • We have added a new iteration of the filter page to this prototype which asks users whether they want to pay, confirm their entitlement or try to see if they have an exemption if they are not sure. If they select pay it will give the user a kickout screen explaining why they cannot pay. If they select confirm their entitlement or im not sure it will take them through the rest of the journey.
  • We have also made a change to the enquiry letter view page to make it less wordy and more contextualised to the situation the user is in and what they need to do next.

What we plan to test:

  • Filter page
  • Partial or full payment
  • New changes to PCN view screen
