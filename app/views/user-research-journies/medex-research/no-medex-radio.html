You did not have a valid medical exemption certificate at the time, but you can still apply

Although you did not have a valid medical exemption certificate when you claimed a free NHS prescription, you still have the chance to apply.

If your application is successful, you will only have to pay the original prescription charge. You will not have to pay your penalty charge.

Important: Important

You only have until 07 June 2023 to get a medical exemption certificate.
