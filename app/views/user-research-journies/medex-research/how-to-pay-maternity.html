You didn't have a valid maternity exemption certificate at the time, but you can still apply

Although you didn't have a valid maternity exemption certificate when you claimed a free prescription, you still have the chance to apply.

Pay the prescription charge now

You will need to pay the original £35.20 prescription charge.

When asked to enter the amount you are paying, you just need to type £17.60 (not the full Penalty Charge Notice amount)

Pay now

Apply for a maternity exemption certificate

Information:

You only have until the 07 May 2023 to get a maternity exemption certificate.

Ask your doctor, midwife or health visitor how to apply.

You can only apply for a maternity exemption certificate if you:

  • were pregnant at the time you claimed the free prescription (the date on your Penalty Charge Notice)
  • you had given birth in the previous 12 months

What happens next?

After your maternity exemption certificate application has been submitted, an automatic check is carried out. You do not need to telephone us.

If your application has been successful and you have paid the £17.60 your Penalty Charge Notice will be closed. You don't need to do anything more.

Information:

If you are not eligible for a maternity exemption certificate you will need to pay the full amount on the Penalty Charge Notice.
