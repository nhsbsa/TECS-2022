Confirm your entitlement

What happens next?

To try to confirm you were entitled to free NHS Prescriptions, we’ll:

  • ask for some details about your exemption, benefit or prepayment certificate
  • check for other reasons you may have been entitled to free NHS prescriptions
  • tell you how to send us proof you were entitled to free NHS prescriptions if we need this
  • let you know the next steps
Information:

If we can confirm you were entitled we will close your case and there will be no further action needed.

If we cannot confirm you were entitled you will be sent a Penalty Charge Notice. This will include information on how and when to pay.
