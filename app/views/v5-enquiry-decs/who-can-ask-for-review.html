Asking for a review

We understand that you may have made an honest mistake and did not mean to do anything wrong.

If you have an exceptional reason why you claimed free or reduced cost NHS dental treatment you were not entitled to, you can ask for a review.

Reasons we will not usually accept

We will not usually review your enquiry letter if:

  • your exemption certificate had expired
  • you feel that you were misadvised by your dental practice
  • you thought that your benefit entitled you to claim

What to do next

If you have an exceptional reason why you made the claim at the time, contact us to review your enquiry letter (opens in a new window).

When you contact us you must include your:

  • reference number 11193492846
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • address including postcode

If you're not entitled to a review

You will be sent a Penalty Charge Notice. This will include information on how and when to pay your penalty charge.
