Not entitled to free NHS prescriptions

If you were not entitled to free NHS prescriptions you will be sent a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN).

We send the enquiry letter before the PCN to give you time to confirm you were entitled to free NHS prescriptions which can stop a PCN being issued.

If you do not confirm you were entitled to free NHS prescriptions within 28 days (by {{ 28 | alterTodaysDateByNumberOfDays }}) we will send a PCN.
