What happens next?

We'll try to confirm you were entitled to free NHS prescriptions.

If we can confirm you were entitled, we'll close your case and no further action will be needed.

If we cannot confirm you were entitled, you'll be sent Penalty Charge Notice.

To try and confirm you were entitled we'll:

  • ask for some details about your benefit, exemption or prepayment certificate
  • check for other reasons you may have been entitled to free NHS prescriptions
  • tell you how to send us proof you were entitled to free NHS prescriptions if we need this
